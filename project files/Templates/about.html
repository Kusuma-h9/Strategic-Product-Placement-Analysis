About Our Project

The Strategic Product Placement Analysis project focuses on understanding how product positioning, pricing strategies, promotions, and customer demographics impact overall sales performance.

Business owners can use this system to identify high-performing product categories, optimize shelf placements, and implement effective promotional strategies. By analyzing real-time sales trends, companies can maximize profit and reduce unnecessary inventory costs.

Our Tableau dashboards provide interactive insights that help decision-makers track performance metrics, compare competitor pricing, and understand customer behavior patterns.

This data-driven approach enables retailers to make smarter decisions, increase customer satisfaction, and ultimately boost revenue and long-term profitability.
